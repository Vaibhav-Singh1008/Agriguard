Modernize Farming

Bridging Agriculture and Technology

Empowering Indian farmers with Artificial Intelligence and modern data analytics to secure our agricultural future.

The Problem

Traditional farming often relies on guesswork to identify crop diseases and manage soil health, leading to lower yields, financial losses, and soil degradation.

Our Solution

AgriGuard provides instantaneous AI-based disease detection and smart soil advice, helping farmers make informed decisions to increase output and enhance soil fertility securely.

Who We Are

AgriGuard is a 2nd-semester Capstone Project for the AI & Cybersecurity program at IIT Patna, aiming to create scalable and secure digital tools for the agricultural sector.
